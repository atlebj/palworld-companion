# Patch v0.1.5 Meta Analysis

The recent v0.1.5 update brought significant balance changes, shaking up the late-game meta.

## Key Changes

### Fire Pal Nerf
- **Reduced Burn Damage**: Burn ticks now deal less % HP damage per tick.
- **Affected Pals**: Jormuntide Ignis, Blazamut, Faleris.

### Why it Matters
Previously, applying Burn was the dominant strategy for boss melting due to % max HP damage. With the nerf, raw DPS becomes more viable again.

### Adaptation Strategy
- **Shift to Element Specific**: Use type advantage more strictly (Water > Fire > Grass > Ground > Electric > Water).
- **Poison is Back**: Poison ticks were untouched, making **Menasting** or **Shadowbeak** with poison skills highly valuable.
- **Frostallion**: Ice remains a strong control element.

## New Feature: Oil Rig Stronghold
The new Oil Rig raid brings unique challenges. High-level Electric Pals like **Orserk** are now S-tier for stunning the heavy machinery enemies.

## Summary
While Fire Pals are still strong, they are no longer the universal answer. Diversify your team with Poison and high raw DPS options.
