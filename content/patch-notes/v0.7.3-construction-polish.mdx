# v0.7.3 — Construction Polish

Released April 2026. No new Pals, no new region, no sweeping mechanic overhauls. This patch fixes two long-standing construction bugs that have annoyed serious base-builders since launch.

## What's new

- **Blueprints stored in chests are now usable for crafting.** Previously, blueprints had to be in your personal inventory to count. This is the QoL change of the patch — finally you can stockpile blueprints in a workshop chest.
- **Floating-roof bug fixed.** Non-foundation supports (pillars, beams) used to leave roofs hovering in mid-air when the support was removed. Roofs now correctly fall or refuse placement.
- General construction polish: snap-tolerance improvements, fewer phantom collisions, smoother foundation grid alignment.

## Meta shift

### Workshop bases are practical now

If you've been running a multi-base setup (main base + [cake farm](/guides/building/cake-egg-farm) + [defense base](/guides/building/defense-base)), the blueprint storage fix lets you keep schematics on the base where they're used. No more inventory-management theater every time you fast-travel.

### Vertical builds become reliable

The floating-roof bug made multi-story bases a gamble — a single misplaced pillar could leave your second story floating ominously. Vertical layouts are now actually usable, opening up build patterns the [Building Guides](/guides/building) didn't recommend before.

### No combat impact

Nothing here touches damage numbers, Pal stats, raid mechanics, or capture rates. The [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) doesn't shift. Combat-only players can skip this entry.

## Recommended adaptation

1. **Move your blueprint stash to a chest** at whichever base you craft from. Free up inventory weight.
2. **Audit any half-built vertical bases** — the patch may auto-correct floating structures, sometimes by dropping them.
3. **Don't rush to update mods.** Construction-related mods may need rebuilds against the new snap logic.
