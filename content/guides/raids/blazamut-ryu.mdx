# Blazamut Ryu Strategy

**Blazamut Ryu** is the powerful "Eternal Flame" raid boss introduced in the Sakurajima update. This fight tests your preparation against both Fire/Dragon and Electric types.

## Boss Stats

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP (Raid)** | 520,000 |
| **Element** | Fire / Dragon (Phase 1) → Electric (Phase 2) |
| **Weakness** | Ice / Water (Phase 1) → Ground (Phase 2) |
| **Level** | 55 |

## The Fight

Blazamut Ryu hits hard with Fire and Dragon moves, and has a unique mechanic where he becomes **temporarily immortal** near death.

### Phase 1: Fire & Dragon
For the majority of the fight (until ~50,000 HP), Blazamut Ryu is a Fire/Dragon hybrid.
*   **Recommendation:** Use **Water** and **Ice** Pals.
    *   **Jormuntide:** Has massive HP and resists Fire. Best overall tank/DPS.
    *   **Azurobe:** Water/Dragon typing gives double resistance to Fire.
    *   **Frostallion:** Legendary Ice moves deal significant damage.

### Phase 2: Electric Resurrection
When his HP drops to ~50k, he triggers a self-heal (recovering ~150k HP) and shifts his element to **Electric**.
*   **Danger:** Water Pals (like Jormuntide) are now **weak** to Electric attacks and will die quickly.
*   **Counter:** Recall your Water types and deploy **Ground** types immediately.
    *   **Anubis:** Fast, high DPS Ground attacks.
    *   **Warsect:** Ground/Grass (Grass resists Electric).

## Optimal Team Comp

| Phase | Pal | Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1 (Fire/Drag)** | **Jormuntide** | Primary Tank & DPS. Resists Fire. |
| **Phase 1 (Fire/Drag)** | **Azurobe** | Secondary Tank. Double resists Fire. |
| **Phase 2 (Elec)** | **Anubis** | Primary Ground DPS. Dodges well. |
| **Phase 2 (Elec)** | **Menasting** | Ground/Dark. Good bulk. |

## Preparation Checklist
1.  **Level:** Ensure all participating Pals are Level 55.
2.  **Souls:** Fully soul-boost HP and Attack.
3.  **Food:** Buff your Pals with **Eikthyrdeer Loco Moco** (+20% Attack) before summoning.
4.  **Gear:** Wear the **Multiclimate Undershirt +2** (drops from Bellanoir Libero) to handle the heat/cold effects if applicable, though primarily use Plasteel Armor for raw defense.
