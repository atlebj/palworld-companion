# Bellanoir Libero Raid Strategy

**Bellanoir Libero** is the entry point to raid content — easier than [Bellanoir Libero Ultra](/guides/raids/bellanoir-libero-ultra) but still a real fight if you're underprepared. This guide is the prep checklist and tactical breakdown.

## Boss Stats

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP (Raid)** | 500,000 |
| **Element** | Dark |
| **Weakness** | Dragon |
| **Time Limit** | 10 Minutes |
| **Recommended Level** | 50 (4★ condensed) |

Unlike the Ultra variant, base Libero **does not phase-shift to Ice**. She stays Dark for the entire fight. That makes the prep much simpler — you bring Dragon Pals and never have to swap.

## The Fight

Libero's signature moves are her **wide-area dark beams** (pierce through everything, including walls) and her **summoned dark orbs** that home in on the closest target. Both are dodgeable on foot but punish Pal positioning.

### Movement is the survival mechanic

If your Pals clump up, one beam wipes the entire team. Position them at angles to her — the Palworld Pal AI doesn't dodge well, so your only lever is initial placement.

### Player damage matters more than you'd think

Your Pals provide consistent DPS, but a [Legendary Rocket Launcher](/items) or a Plasteel melee variant from [v0.7.2](/patch-notes/v0.7.2-melee-rarity) lets you contribute 20-30% of the damage. At the DPS threshold for the 10-minute timer, that 20-30% is the difference between a kill and a wipe.

The Gobfin stack is the meta — don't skip it. See the [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) for alternatives if you're missing any of these.

## Base Setup

1. **Dedicated raid base.** Do not fight her at your main base — she will destroy work objects within range. The beach south of Mount Obsidian is the classic raid arena.
2. **Stone foundation, no walls.** Walls don't help (her beams pierce) and they break your Pal AI's line of sight.
3. **Summoning altar near the edge** of the arena. Gives you room to back up when she spawns aggressively.
4. **Feed box stocked with Eikthyrdeer Loco Moco** — +20% attack buff before summoning. Cheap, easy, mandatory.

## Common mistakes

- **Bringing Fire types because "she's dark."** Fire is not Dark's weakness — Dragon is. Use the [Type Chart](/guides/type-chart).
- **Skipping the Gobfin stack.** It looks silly. It's correct.
- **Fighting at your main base.** You will rebuild your entire base. Do not skip the dedicated raid arena.

After your first clear, level up to Lv 55 and condense your roster before attempting [Bellanoir Libero Ultra](/guides/raids/bellanoir-libero-ultra) — same Pal, different beast.
