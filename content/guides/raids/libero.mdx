# Bellanoir Libero Raid Strategy

Bellanoir Libero is one of the toughest raid bosses in Palworld. This guide covers the essential preparation and execution strategies.

## Boss Stats
- **Element**: Dark
- **Weakness**: Dragon
- **Recommended Level**: 50 (Max)

## Recommended Pals
1. **Jetragon**: High Dragon damage and mobility.
2. **Chillet**: Applies Dragon element to your attacks.
3. **Gobfin (x4)**: In party for player damage boost (Vanguard trait).

## Base Preparation
- Build a **Stone Foundation** arena away from your main base to avoid destruction.
- Place **Defense Walls** if possible, though her attacks pierce.
- Use **Condensed** Pals (Rank 4) for maximum stats.

## Phase 1
Use your strongest Dragon Pals. Focus on dodging her wide-area dark beams.

## Phase 2 (If applicable)
Wait for her element shift (she might switch to Ice in higher tiers). If she switches to Ice, swap to Fire Pals like **Blazamut** or **Jormuntide Ignis**.

## Player Gear
- **Legendary Rocket Launcher**: High burst damage.
- **Assault Rifle**: Consistent DPS.
- **Pal Metal Armor**: Highest defense available.
