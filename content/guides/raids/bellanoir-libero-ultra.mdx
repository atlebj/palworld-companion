# Bellanoir Libero Ultra Strategy

**Bellanoir Libero Ultra** is the pinnacle of Palworld's raid content (prior to the Sakurajima update). With nearly double the HP of the standard Libero and a devastating phase shift, this fight requires a fully optimized base and roster.

## Boss Stats

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 900,000 |
| **Element** | Dark (Primary) → Ice (Final Phase) |
| **Weakness** | Dragon (Phase 1) → Fire (Phase 2) |
| **Time Limit** | 10 Minutes |
| **Recommended Level** | 50 (Max Condensed) |

## The Mechanics

The fight is a DPS check with a survival twist at the end.

### Phase 1: The Dark Beam Barrage
For the first ~90% of her health bar (approx. 810k HP), Bellanoir Libero spams wide-area Dark attacks, including her signature **Nightmare Bloom**, which fires a massive beam that tracks targets.

**Strategy:**
- **Dragon Type Pals** are essential for dealing super-effective damage.
- **Spread Out:** Her beams pierce and have a wide hitbox. If your Pals are clumped, they will all die at once.

### Phase 2: The Ice Shift
Once she drops below ~10% HP (around 90,000 HP left), she will **change her element to Ice**.
- **Danger:** Your Dragon Pals (which you likely brought for Phase 1) are now weak to her Ice attacks and will be frozen/wiped instantly.
- **Counter:** You must recall your Dragon Pals and deploy **Fire Pals** or **Dark/Fire** hybrids immediately.

## Base Setup
1.  **Location:** Build a dedicated raid base on flat terrain (e.g., the beach south of Obsidian Mountain). Do **not** fight her in your main base; she will destroy everything.
2.  **Structure:**
    *   **Palbox:** Place it in the center on a raised stone foundation.
    *   **Walls?** No. Her attacks pierce walls. Open space is better for AI pathing.
    *   **Food/Beds:** Minimal. Just a feed box near the spawn.
3.  **Summoning:** Place the Summoning Altar near the edge of the circle so you have room to back up.
