# Automated Ore Farm

Building a dedicated Ore base is crucial for mid-to-late game progression. Here is a blueprint for a high-efficiency farm.

## Location
Find a spot with at least **6-8 Ore Nodes**. Popular locations include:
- **Desolate Church** (Early game)
- **Mount Obsidian** (Late game, includes Coal)

## Pal Recommendations
- **Digtoise**: High mining speed, especially when condensed.
- **Anubis**: Excellent mining and transport.
- **Astegon**: Best for pure mining power.

## Layout Tips
1. **Foundation**: Build a flat platform over uneven terrain to ensure Pals can navigate.
2. **Beds & Feed Box**: Place them centrally to minimize travel time.
3. **Chests**: Place a chest right next to the Palbox for quick fast-travel transfers.

## Automation
Ensure you have enough **Transport** Pals (like Killamari or Helzephyr) to move the ore from the nodes to the chest. If using Digtoise, its mining spin attack drops ore on the ground, so transport is key.
