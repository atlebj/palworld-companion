# Automated Ore Farm

A dedicated ore base is the unlock that lets you scale your mid-game crafting. Refined Metal Ingots, Plasteel, and almost every weapon schematic gate behind ore throughput. This is the build.

## Why a separate base?

Mining and handiwork conflict. If your miners and crafters share a base, miners idle waiting for transport Pals, and crafters can't keep up with raw ore intake. Splitting them lets each base specialize.

## Location

Find a spot with at least **6-8 Ore Nodes** clustered close enough for a single foundation to cover them. Popular picks:

| Location | When | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Desolate Church** | Lv 10-25 | Easy access, no hostile NPCs nearby. Limited node density. |
| **Mount Obsidian** | Lv 25-40 | Includes coal nodes. Hot biome — need [hot resistance gear](/items). |
| **Verdant Brook** | Lv 30+ | High node density. Some pals in the area are aggressive. |
| **Ice Wind Island** | Lv 40+ | Premium node count. Need cold resistance. Best for endgame. |

Use the [Resource Map](/map) to scout candidates before committing.

## Pal Recommendations

| Role | Pal | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mining (primary)** | [Anubis](/pals/Anubis) | Lv3 mining + S-tier melee. Doubles as base defense. |
| **Mining (specialist)** | [Astegon](/pals/Astegon) | Lv4 mining. Pure throughput. Slow to acquire — bred from late-game lineage. |
| **Mining (mid-game)** | [Digtoise](/pals/Digtoise) | Spins on nodes, dropping ore. Less efficient than Anubis but available much earlier. |
| **Transport** | [Wumpo](/pals/Wumpo) | Lv4 transport. Moves dropped ore to the chest before it despawns. |
| **Transport (alt)** | [Killamari](/pals/Killamari) | Lv1 transport. Cheap, available early. Use until you breed something better. |
| **Defense** | [Blazamut](/pals/Blazamut) | Lv4 mining AND 150 melee — does the job and fights raids alone. |

(See the [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) for the full ranking of mining Pals.)

## Layout

1. **Foundation over the node cluster.** Flat tiles let miners path to every node. Without foundation, uneven terrain blocks half your crew.
2. **Logging Camp wood chest off to one side**, ore chest near the Palbox. Two chests prevents transport Pals from idling waiting for stack-merge.
3. **Beds and feed box centered** so SAN and hunger top-ups don't pull workers across the map.
4. **One repair bench** — high-tier mining Pals chew through their pickaxes. Repair-on-the-spot beats fast-travel for tool maintenance.
5. **No kindling, no handiwork.** Resist mixing jobs. (See the [Logging & Plantation guide](/guides/building/logging-plantation) for why hybrid bases work for SOME job combos but not this one.)

## Throughput

A 6-Pal mining crew on an 8-node cluster produces roughly:

| Crew composition | Ore/hour |
| :--- | :--- |
| 2× Anubis + 2× Digtoise + 2× Wumpo | ~600 |
| 3× Astegon + 3× Wumpo (endgame) | ~900 |
| 1× Blazamut + 2× Anubis + 3× Wumpo | ~750 (plus raid defense) |

Compare that to a hand-mining session: even with the [Refined Metal Pickaxe](/items), a player chains roughly 80 ore/hour. The base is ~10× better.

## Common mistakes

- **Putting the ore farm in your main base.** Miners idle while transport Pals handle handiwork queues. Productivity collapses by ~40%.
- **Forgetting SAN management.** A high-output mining crew bottoms out SAN in 6-8 hours. Pair this base with the [Hot Spring & SAN Recovery](/guides/building/hot-spring-base) base for rotation.
- **No defense Pal.** Hostile NPCs and wildlife raids will destroy a defenseless ore base. Even one combat Pal makes the difference.
- **Trying to do coal AND ore from one base.** Coal nodes appear on Mount Obsidian alongside ore. The crew can theoretically work both — but the transport queue gets overwhelmed. Build a separate coal base later.

## Connect it to your other bases

- **Ore chest** → fast-travel to your crafting base at the end of each in-game day.
- **Sell excess refined metal** to merchants for gold to fund the [Endgame Checklist](/guides/progression/endgame-checklist).
- **Use the [Workforce Composition Planner](/calculators/base-efficiency)** to verify your crew has zero skill gaps before committing to a layout.
