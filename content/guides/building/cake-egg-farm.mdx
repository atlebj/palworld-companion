# Cake & Egg Farm

If you've used the [Breeding Pathfinder](/calculators/breeding) you know one breeding pair eats a **[Cake](/items/cake)** every cycle. Run two pairs in parallel and your cake supply collapses inside an hour. This base solves that.

## What cake actually needs

| Ingredient | Qty | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flour** | 5 | Mill (wheat seeds + farm) |
| **Red Berries** | 8 | Berry plantation |
| **Honey** | 2 | Beegarde / Cinnamoth ranch |
| **Milk** | 7 | Mozzarina ranch |
| **Eggs** | 8 | Chikipi ranch |

Every cake takes ~30 minutes of crafting at a Cooking Pot. The bottleneck is almost always **milk and eggs** because ranch outputs are slow.

## Pal Recommendations

| Role | Pal | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Farming (wheat, berries)** | [Verdash](/pals/Verdash) | Lv2 planting + Lv3 gathering — works the whole plant cycle alone. |
| **Watering** | [Jormuntide](/pals/Jormuntide) | Lv4 watering keeps every plot fed. |
| **Ranching** | [Mozzarina](/pals/Mozzarina), [Chikipi](/pals/Chikipi), [Beegarde](/pals/Beegarde) | One of each, working their respective drops. |
| **Cooking** | [Wixen](/pals/Wixen) | Lv2 kindling + Lv3 handiwork — operates the cooking pot. |
| **Transport** | [Wumpo](/pals/Wumpo) | Lv4 transport keeps the chest stocked. |

## Layout

1. **Three plots:** wheat field, red berry plantation, separate row for honey/milk/eggs ranchers.
2. **Cooking Pot adjacent to feed box** so your handiwork Pal doesn't waste time walking ingredients across the base.
3. **One chest per ingredient class** until you unlock storage upgrades — cuts down on pickup paths.
4. **Foundation everything.** Ranches and plots on raw terrain confuse pathing.

## Throughput

A clean four-Pal kitchen produces roughly **2 cakes/hour** running unattended. That's enough to keep two breeding pairs busy without intervention. Add a second Wixen and a second Mozzarina and you double output.

## Common mistakes

- **Putting the breeding farm in the same base.** Pals will wander between jobs. Build cake here, breed two map tiles away.
- **Forgetting flour.** Wheat seeds are sold by the Small Settlement merchant for 100 gold each — cheaper than farming them out from scratch.
- **Overstacking ranches.** Three ranchers max per base; more and they fight over the same feed box.
