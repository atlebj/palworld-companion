# Endgame Checklist (Level 50)

Hitting Level 50 is the start of endgame, not the finish line. There's a real loot grind, several optional bosses, and meta-defining items still ahead. Here's the punch list.

## Step 1: condense your core team to 4★

Capture 4 copies of each of your main Pals and condense them at the Pal Essence Condenser. A 4★ condensed Pal has:
- **+25% HP**
- **+25% attack**
- **+25% defense**

That's a flat 25% combat power increase before anything else changes. Priority order:

1. **[Anubis](/pals/Anubis)** — does work AND fights. Universal pick.
2. **[Jormuntide](/pals/Jormuntide)** or **[Jormuntide_Ignis](/pals/Jormuntide_Ignis)** — your boss DPS.
3. **[Frostallion_Noct](/pals/Frostallion_Noct)** — best tank in the dex.
4. **[Paladius](/pals/Paladius)** — defensive raid anchor.

(See the [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) for which Pal fits which role.)

## Step 2: passive grind

Run your top combat Pals through the breeding system to roll **legendary passives**:

| Passive | Effect | Why it matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lucky** | +15% attack and work speed | Hardest to roll; biggest upgrade. |
| **Musclehead** | +30% melee, -50% attack speed | Better than it looks on bossing. |
| **Ferocious** | +20% attack | Common but stacks. |
| **Burly Body** | +20% defense | For tanks. |

Use the [Stat Planner](/calculators/iv) to compare loadouts and the [Breeding Pathfinder](/calculators/breeding) to find the cheapest path to a target Pal.

## Step 3: gear up

Late-game gear progression:

| Slot | Best at 50 | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Armor** | Pal Metal Armor | Crafted; Ancient Civ Parts gate |
| **Helmet** | Multiclimate Undershirt +2 | [Bellanoir Libero](/guides/raids/libero) drop |
| **Weapon (raid)** | Legendary Rocket Launcher | Boss drop / Lifmunk effigy reward |
| **Weapon (mob)** | Legendary Assault Rifle | Schematic + crafting |
| **Sphere** | Legendary Sphere | Crafted; needed for ALL late captures |

The **Legendary Sphere** recipe is gated behind Ancient Civ Parts. Farm those at the **Investigator** dungeons (level 40+).

## Step 4: raid bosses, in order

1. **[Bellanoir Libero](/guides/raids/libero)** — entry-level raid. Drops the Multiclimate Undershirt.
2. **[Blazamut Ryu](/guides/raids/blazamut-ryu)** — Sakurajima content. Drops Blazamut Ryu's missile launcher.
3. **[Bellanoir Libero Ultra](/guides/raids/bellanoir-libero-ultra)** — pinnacle pre-Sakurajima content. 900k HP, phase change.

Beat all three and you've cleared current raid content.

## Step 5: base optimization

At Lv 50 you should be running **three bases minimum**:

1. **Main production** — mining + handiwork. (See the [Ore Farm](/guides/building/ore-farm) guide.)
2. **Cake & Egg Farm** — keeps breeding running. (See the [Cake Farm](/guides/building/cake-egg-farm) guide.)
3. **Combat Pal rest base** — SAN recovery. (See the [Hot Spring](/guides/building/hot-spring-base) guide.)

Use the [Workforce Composition Planner](/calculators/base-efficiency) to make sure each base's Pal crew has zero work-skill gaps.

## What's left after the checklist?

- **PvP** if you're on a server.
- **Pal Effigy collection** for permanent capture-rate bonuses.
- **Dungeon speedrunning** for the cosmetic schematics.
- **Sakurajima exploration** — the DLC adds an entire region of Pals and boss content.

When the next major patch lands, regenerate the [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) (we use a [data-driven skill](https://github.com/) to keep it honest) and check [Patch Notes](/patch-notes) for meta shifts.
