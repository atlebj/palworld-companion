# Oil Rig Stronghold Guide

Two Oil Rigs exist in Palworld. They look similar, drop similar-looking schematics, and confuse a lot of guides — so let's start by separating them.

## The two rigs

| Rig | Update | Location | Level | Boss |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sakurajima Oil Rig** | Sakurajima Update (2024) | Coords **538, -421**, SE of Marsh Island (430, -345) | Enemies up to Lv 55 | No dedicated boss |
| **Rayne Syndicate Platform** | [Feybreak Update](/patch-notes/v0.4.11-feybreak), Dec 23 2024 | Ocean **west of Feybreak Island** | Enemies up to Lv 60 | Attack Chopper (~1.3M HP) |

Neither rig has a fast-travel point. Both require a flying or swimming mount to reach.

The Sakurajima rig is the entry-level grind. The Rayne Syndicate Platform is the current endgame farm — that's where the meta-defining Feybreak weapon schematics drop.

---

## Sakurajima Oil Rig (Lv 50-55)

Use this rig if you're still gearing up. Drops crude oil, gold, ammo, and a chance at Old World legendary schematics from the big chest. Resource-efficient but no Feybreak weapons.

### Approach
- Flying mount (Jetragon, Faleris, etc.) recommended. Bring a swimming Pal as backup since lasers can knock you out of the sky.
- The rig defends with turrets and laser cannons. Hostile humans patrol with assault rifles, rocket launchers, and missile launchers.

### Loot

The big chest's location is randomized per session. Walk the rig once to find it. Common drops include:
- Crude Oil (large stack)
- Ancient Civilization Parts
- Schematics for [late-game weapons](/items) — Rocket Launcher, Assault Rifle, Laser Rifle, etc., at varying rarities

Chests respawn roughly hourly.

### Loadout
- **Armor:** Legendary Heat or Cold Resistant Pal Metal Armor (same stats — pick based on climate)
- **Weapon:** Legendary Rocket Launcher
- **Pal:** Jetragon for mobility

## Rayne Syndicate Platform (Lv 60 — Feybreak)

This is the rig that matters for endgame. **22 small chests + 1 big chest** (the big chest has 5 possible spawn locations, randomized per session). The big chest is gated by the **Attack Chopper** boss fight.

You can run it **once every 30 minutes** — the timer starts after you've looted every chest.

### Drop rates (Big Chest, Feybreak weapons)

Each tier of each weapon has its own drop chance. Tier 4 schematics are deeply rare:

| Weapon | Tier 2 | Tier 3 | Tier 4 (Legendary) |
| :--- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Multi Guided Missile Launcher | 14.75% | 9.00% | **1.25%** |
| Advanced Bow | 14.75% | 9.00% | **1.25%** |
| Laser Gatling Gun | 14.75% | 9.00% | **1.25%** |
| Plasma Cannon | 14.75% | 9.00% | **1.25%** |

Average runs per Tier-4 Feybreak schematic: **~80 runs**. That drops to ~32 if you use the [Drafting Table](https://palworld.fandom.com/wiki/Drafting_Table) (added in v0.5.0) to merge 5× Tier 3 schematics.

### Drop rates (Small Chests, Hexolite armor)

Each of the 22 small chests has independent rolls. Per-chest rates are tiny but compound across all 22:

| Schematic | Tier 1 | Tier 4 |
| :--- | ---: | ---: |
| Hexolite Helmet | 3.89% | 0.03% |
| Hexolite Armor | 3.89% | 0.03% |
| Heat / Cold Resistant Hexolite Armor | 2.34% | 0.02% |
| Lightweight Hexolite Armor | 1.56% | 0.01% |

Average runs to a Tier-4 Hexolite Armor without the Drafting Table: **~146 runs**.

### The Attack Chopper

The big chest is locked until you kill the Attack Chopper. Stats:
- **HP:** ~1.3 million
- **Attacks:** Machine Gun (continuous), Homing Missiles, Bombing Run
- **XP bonus:** 46× normal

Two viable strategies:
1. **Cover + Multi Guided Missile Launcher** — peek out of cover, fire homing missiles around a pillar, rinse.
2. **Lifesteal build** — Felbat or Lovander partner skill (lifesteal applies to player). With splash damage from a Rocket Launcher or Plasma Cannon, you can heal to full on each shot.

The Tower at Big Chest Location E is the most reliable arena — the chopper's bullets get blocked by the pillar.

### Recommended Pal party

| Pal | Role | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [Felbat](/pals/Felbat) | Lifesteal source | Partner skill gives lifesteal to both Pal and player — required for the lifesteal Chopper strategy. |
| [Galeclaw](/pals/Galeclaw) | Mobility | Grapple-glide between platforms. Critical for the 22-chest loot route. |
| [Gobfin](/pals/Gobfin) × 2-3 | Damage buff | Each Gobfin with **Vanguard + Stronghold Strategist** passives stacks +30% attack, +10% defense for the player. |

(See the [Tier List](/guides/tier-list) for alternatives; these specific picks are because of partner skills, not raw stats.)

### Recommended player loadout

**Weapons:**
- 2× Grappling Guns (one as primary mobility, one as backup)
- [Rocket Launcher](/items) — for stunning groups of NPCs
- Multi Guided Missile Launcher **OR** Plasma Cannon — for the Chopper

**Accessories:**
- Attack Pendant +2
- Attack Pendant +1
- Triple Jump Boots
- Defense Pendant +2
- Climate Undershirt (swap in on approach — cold zone near the rig)

**Food buff:** Mammorest Curry (+25% attack). Mandatory; it's free DPS.

### Enemies you'll fight

The rig is patrolled by 11 distinct human archetypes plus 6 Pal types. The dangerous ones:

| Enemy | Why it's a threat |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Crushers** | x20 HP and x18 damage. Immune to explosive stuns. Guard the big chest. |
| **Shotgun NPC** | x40 damage — they one-shot you when your shield is down. Watch flanks. |
| **Laser Rifle NPC** | Accurate consistent damage. Stacks of them shred shields fast. Priority kills. |
| **Electric Crossbow NPC** | Stun effect. Getting stunned in front of a Laser Rifle is the #1 cause of death on the rig. |
| **Knocklem (Pal)** | 126 base HP and x20.83 health bonus. Immune to Rocket Launcher stuns. Skip rather than fight. |

### The farming loop

1. **Glide or fly in with altitude** so you can spot the big chest from far away.
2. **Go straight to the big chest.** Clear surrounding NPCs first.
3. **Summon the Chopper** by interacting with the big chest. Position matters — see the per-location notes below.
4. **Defeat the Chopper.** Each time you send out a Pal, the Chopper focuses it until you deal damage.
5. **Grapple + Galeclaw** around the rig looting all 22 small chests.
6. **Homeward Thundercloud** back to base when done.

Per-location strategy for the big chest spawn:
- **A (F1):** Wait for the big NPC wave before summoning. Manageable.
- **B (SL):** Sometimes you can kill the Chopper before the wave arrives.
- **C (SL):** Don't fight here — grapple to Location E and fight at the tower.
- **D (SL):** Safe to fight; no NPCs can climb up.
- **E (F1):** Tower fighting platforms work great; the wave can arrive but you're elevated.

### Notes

- **Mods exist** that buff Oil Rig legendary drop rates. The Vanilla drop rates above assume an unmodded game.
- **The Anti-Air Missile Defense can be permanently disabled.** The Lasers respawn after being disabled.
- **Felbat will sometimes teleport to the player** and mid-fight a Crusher. Park them on a high roof during NPC clears.

## Which rig to farm

- **Lv 50-55, gearing up:** Sakurajima Oil Rig. The Old World schematics it drops feed into the [Endgame Checklist](/guides/progression/endgame-checklist) progression.
- **Lv 60, hunting Legendary weapons:** Rayne Syndicate Platform. Every meta DPS player runs this; the Plasma Cannon and Multi Guided Missile Launcher trivialize most raid bosses including [Bellanoir Libero Ultra](/guides/raids/bellanoir-libero-ultra).
- **You've already finished both:** consider the [Tides of Terraria](/patch-notes/v0.6.0-tides-of-terraria) content for fresh endgame.
