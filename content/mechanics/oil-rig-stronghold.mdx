The **Oil Rig Stronghold** remains one of the premier late-game activities for farming **Legendary Schematics** and **Ancient Technology Core fragments**.

Since the **Deep Sea Update** (late 2025), the difficulty has scaled up. The Laser Syndicate now employs level 60 turrets and advanced AI pathing.

## Overview of the Stronghold
- **Location:** Southeast Ocean (Coordinates: 550, -450)
- **Enemy Level:** 55–60 Elite
- **Key Loot:** Legendary Weapon Schematics (Laser Rifle, Rocket Launcher II), Tier 5 Accessories.
- **Refresh Timer:** 30 minutes (real time)

## How to safely approach (2026 update)
Flying directly in is suicide unless you have a **Jetragon with "Stealth Flight"** (a passive added in v1.5).

### The reliable method:
1. **Approach via underwater mount:** Azurobe or Jormuntide can swim under the radar.
2. **Grapple Hook entry:** Once close, grapple up the side pillars to avoid the main turret line of sight.
3. **Disable the generator:** Located in the central control room (see map above). This disables the outer laser turrets for 5 minutes.

## Key loot locations
There are usually **4 main chests** on the rig:

1. **The Helipad Chest (Gold):** Often guarded by a Crusher heavy unit.
2. **The Cargo Bay Chest (Purple):** Hidden behind containers on the lower deck.
3. **The Central Command Chest (Big Gold):** Inside the main building, usually requires killing the commander.
4. **The Underwater Cache:** *New in 2026 update* - Look for bubbles near the rig's support legs.

> **Warning:** The chests are trapped. Bring a **Trap Disarm Kit** (crafted at workbench level 50).

## Recommended loadouts
- **Weapon:** Rocket Launcher (for AoE clearing grouped enemies) + Laser Rifle (for sniping turrets)
- **Armor:** Pal Metal Armor (Cold/Heat Resistant depending on time of day)
- **Essential Pals:**
  - **Galeclaw:** For mobility/gliding between platforms.
  - **Lyleen:** For massive AoE healing during intense firefights.
  - **Orserk:** Deals double damage to water/electric enemies commonly found on the rig.
