These calculators help you make **better decisions** without memorizing formulas or guessing how mechanics interact.

They are designed as **decision helpers**, not exact probability simulators.

## Available calculators

### Capture Chance Helper
Estimate whether a capture attempt is worth trying and what to improve before throwing.

- Factors considered: HP %, status effects, sphere tier, target difficulty
- Best used together with the explanation guide

→ [Open Capture Chance Helper](/calculators/capture-chance)  
→ [Read Capture Chance explained](/mechanics/capture-chance)

### Work Speed Estimator
Compare uptime, task switching, and raw Work Speed bonuses to see what actually improves base productivity.

- Helps explain why layout and roles matter more than stats
- Useful when optimizing mid- to late-game bases

→ [Open Work Speed Estimator](/calculators/work-speed)  
→ [Read Work Speed explained](/mechanics/work-speed)
