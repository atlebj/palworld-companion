These calculators help you make **better decisions** without memorizing formulas or guessing how mechanics interact.

They are designed as **decision helpers**, not exact probability simulators. The site's [FAQ](/faq) covers accuracy and limitations for each tool.

## Available calculators

### Capture Chance Helper
Estimate whether a capture attempt is worth trying and what to improve before throwing.

- Factors: HP %, status effects, sphere tier, target level
- Best paired with the [capture chance explainer](/mechanics/capture-chance)

→ [Open Capture Chance Helper](/calculators/capture-chance)

### Damage Calculator
Estimate damage from a player or Pal attack against a specific target.

- Factors: base attack, defense, level scaling, type matchups
- Honest disclaimer: passive interactions and crit variance introduce error
- Read the [damage formula deep-dive](/mechanics/damage-formula) for the math

→ [Open Damage Calculator](/calculators/damage)

### Work Speed Estimator
Compare uptime, task switching, and Work Speed bonuses to see what actually improves base productivity.

- Helps explain why layout and roles matter more than raw stats
- Useful when optimizing mid- to late-game bases

→ [Open Work Speed Estimator](/calculators/work-speed)

### Stat Planner (IVs, Condense, Passives)
Compare Pal stat loadouts forward. Pick IVs, condense rank, and passive skills; see projected HP/ATK/DEF.

- Does **not** reverse-engineer hidden IVs (the game doesn't expose them)
- The right tool for "should I condense this Pal now or wait for a better roll?"

→ [Open Stat Planner](/calculators/iv)

### Breeding Pathfinder
Find the shortest breeding chain to a target Pal from your current collection.

- Backed by a 9,453-pair lookup table (community datamined)
- Tree View visualizes the entire lineage
- Use the [My Pals](/my-pals) box to feed your saved roster in

→ [Open Breeding Pathfinder](/calculators/breeding)

### Breeding Simulator
Simulate any pair to see what they produce, or reverse-search "what two parents make this child?"

- Forward and reverse lookups
- Combined with the Pathfinder, this covers every breeding question worth asking

→ [Open Breeding Simulator](/calculators/breeding-simulator)

### Workforce Composition Planner (Base Efficiency)
Plan a base crew and see where work-skill coverage is concentrated — and where you have gaps.

- Reports relative coverage rather than fake hourly numbers
- Pair with the [worker roles guide](/bases/worker-roles) to understand what skills matter where

→ [Open Workforce Composition Planner](/calculators/base-efficiency)

## How to use these tools effectively

Calculators work best when combined with the guides:

- **Base optimization** → [Worker roles](/bases/worker-roles) and [Pathing & AI](/bases/pathing-ai)
- **Breeding strategy** → [Passive skills](/breeding/passive-skills), [Trait inheritance](/breeding/trait-inheritance)
- **Combat planning** → [Damage formula](/mechanics/damage-formula), [Tier list](/guides/tier-list)
- **Progression** → [Early game](/guides/progression/early-game) and [Endgame checklist](/guides/progression/endgame-checklist)

The goal is not perfect numbers — it's **predictable results**.
