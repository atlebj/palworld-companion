# Content and Design Suggestions

Based on a review of popular Palworld resources like PalworldTrainer and Paldb.cc, here are comprehensive suggestions for content, tools, and design elements to incorporate into our site.

## 1. Content Categories

To provide a complete resource, the site should cover the following primary categories:

### Pal Information (The "Paldeck")
*   **Comprehensive Stats:** Base stats (HP, Attack, Defense), growth rates, and max level stats.
*   **Skills & Abilities:**
    *   **Active Skills:** Move name, element type, power, cooldown, and description.
    *   **Passive Skills:** Buffs/debuffs and their statistical impact.
    *   **Partner Skills:** The Pal's unique utility (e.g., flying mount, life steal, gathering bonus).
*   **Work Suitability:** Clearly list what tasks a Pal can do (Kindling, Watering, Mining, etc.) and at what level.
*   **Drop Items:** What items the Pal drops when defeated or captured.
*   **Habitat/Location:** Maps showing day and night spawn locations.

### Game Mechanics & Guides
*   **Breeding:** A cornerstone feature. Explain the inheritance mechanics (IVs, Passives) and how parent combinations work.
*   **Status Effects:** Details on hunger, injuries (sprain, fracture), and mood (depressed), and how to cure them.
*   **Player Stats & Progression:** Leveling guide, technology points, and stat allocation recommendations.

### Items, Structures, & Technologies
*   **Equipment:** Weapons, armor, spheres, gliders, and accessories with their crafting requirements and stats.
*   **Consumables:** Food, medicine, and skill fruits.
*   **Base Building:** Production structures, Pal beds, defensive installations, and decorative furniture.
*   **Technology Tree:** A visual layout of what unlocks at each level.

## 2. Tools & Calculators

Interactive tools are essential for user retention and utility:

*   **Breeding Calculator:**
    *   *Direct:* Select Parent A + Parent B = Resulting Pal.
    *   *Reverse (Pathfinder):* Select desired Pal -> Show all Parent combinations that result in it.
    *   *Chain Breeding:* Tools to calculate multi-generational breeding to pass down specific passive skills.
*   **Interactive Map:**
    *   Toggleable markers for: Fast Travel statues, Dungeons, Syndicate Camps, Skill Fruit Trees, Lifmunk Effigies, Alpha Bosses, and specific Pal spawns.
*   **IV (Individual Value) Calculator:** A tool to input a Pal's level and stats to determine its hidden IV multipliers.
*   **Base Planner / Resource Calculator:** Tools to calculate the materials needed for a specific build or the output rate of a base setup.
*   **Tier Lists:** Community or data-driven tier lists for Best Combat Pals, Best Base Workers, and Best Mounts.

## 3. Graphics & UI/UX Suggestions

A clean, game-themed aesthetic will improve the user experience:

### Iconography
*   **Standardized Game Icons:** Use high-quality icons directly from or heavily inspired by the game for:
    *   *Elements:* Fire, Water, Grass, Electric, Ground, Ice, Dragon, Dark, Neutral.
    *   *Work Suitability:* The specific UI icons for Kindling, Handiwork, Mining, etc.
    *   *Items:* Spheres, materials, food.
*   **Visual Hierarchy:** Ensure that elemental typing and work suitability are immediately recognizable via color-coded badges or icons.

### Layout & Formatting
*   **Data Grids & Tables:** Use sortable, filterable tables for lists of Pals, Skills, and Items. Users need to sort by "Highest Attack" or filter by "Mining Lv 3".
*   **Card-Based UI:** For the Paldeck, a grid of cards showing the Pal's image, name, element icon(s), and a quick summary of its best work suitability.
*   **Tooltips:** Implement rich tooltips. Hovering over a passive skill name in a table should show its description without requiring a page navigation.
*   **Dark Mode:** A dark theme (like the one currently implemented: Background `#0f1115`, Card `#1a1f2b`) is standard for gaming wikis and databases. Maintain high contrast for text.

### Media
*   **High-Resolution Assets:** Use clean, transparent PNGs or WebP images for Pal renders and items.
*   **Interactive Elements:** Ensure calculators and maps feel responsive and snappy.

By implementing these suggestions, the site will serve as a comprehensive, user-friendly companion for Palworld players.
